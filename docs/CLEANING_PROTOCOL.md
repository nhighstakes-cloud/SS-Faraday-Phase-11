# Phase 12.1: UHV Substrate Cleaning Protocol

To maintain a 12.3nm Casimir Gap, surfaces must be **atomically clean**.

### 1. EX-SITU: CO2 Snow Cleaning
* **Purpose:** Remove particulates and loose hydrocarbons.
* **Method:** Dry, non-contact spray. Leaves zero residue.

### 2. WET-CLEAN: Modified Piranha Etch
* **Solution:** Ammonium Persulfate + H2SO4.
* **Purpose:** Removes organics and non-diamond carbon.
* **Rinse:** Triple-DI Water + Electronic Grade Isopropanol (IPA).

### 3. IN-SITU: Ar+ Cluster Ion Milling
* **Location:** Inside Vacuum Gantry.
* **Purpose:** Final atomic-scale cleaning before gap closure.
* **Follow-up:** 150°C UHV Bakeout for 4 hours.
